Style Guide:

Colors:

  • #FF8C00 || rgb(255, 140, 0) || orange color
  • #808000 || rgb(128, 128, 0) || olive color
  • #0000CD || rgb(0, 0, 205) || blue color
  • #5DA800 || rgb(93, 168, 0) || green color
  • #E8D100 || rgb(232, 209, 0) || yellow color

Fonts:

  • Regular font: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;
  • Table & form font: "Arial Black", Gadget, sans-serif;

I chose the color scheme early on, by just playing around with the color websites listed on blackboard. It took me awhile, but I found a combination of color's that I liked the best. As for the fonts, I looked at some examples online and thought that the ones I chose would be best for my website.
